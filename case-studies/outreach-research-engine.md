# Outreach Research Engine — Verified outreach preparation

## Product problem

Outbound work is strongest when research, qualification, and message preparation are connected—but weak evidence must not silently become send-ready claims.

## System design

- Cloudflare Workers-based workflow for company discovery and public-evidence review.
- Structured readiness criteria before a lead can move to a prepared outreach state.
- Import/export safety and browser-storage resilience for operator-facing workflows.
- Static build and browser-level verification for the delivery surface.

## Engineering emphasis

The product models uncertainty as a first-class state. Evidence gates, safe imports, and security headers make the workflow reliable enough for real outbound preparation while retaining human review for claims and messaging.

## Public boundary

Prospect lists, research records, campaign content, and source code remain private.
